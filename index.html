What are Dero Girls?

Dero Girls is a collection of 1,000 unique NFTs. Some Dero Girls are rarer than others, but It is mainly about what YOU like. This project is built on top of the Dero blockchain, which is very secure and makes your NFT private. Holding a Dero Girl NFT will make you viable for future airdrops of tokens and NFTs built on top of Dero. All funds that are made will be used to support, sponsor, and invest in the Dero ecosystem and reword holders.
